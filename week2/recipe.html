Recipe

Prahok Ktiss

Ingredients:

  • Prahok(2-3 tbsp)
  • Minced pork(100-150g)
  • Coconut milk(1 cup)
  • Eggs(1-2)
  • Kroeung
  • Garlic(2-3 cloves)
  • Shallots(2-3)
  • Chili
  • sugar
  • Fish sauce
steps:
    1. Prepare ingredients
    2. Cook the pork
    3. Add kroeung
    4. Add prahok
    5. Add coconut milk
    6. Add eggs
    7. Add sugar and chili
    8. Serve warm with fresh vegetables

Calories Protein Fat
